# Elev8 Centers — Q2 2026 Strategic Direction

**Client:** Elev8 Centers (Harlem NY pin) | **Tier:** 1 Recovery
**Prepared:** 2026-05-18 | **Author:** Aleksandar (HQDM Search Intelligence)
**Scope:** Harlem NY pin only.

---

## TL;DR

Elev8's Harlem pin is the **only competitor in NYC addiction-treatment** that gained ground on every single one of 8 tracked Maps queries over the last 3 months — +787 net cell-positions across the 140-cell grid, zero losses. Organically, Elev8 **owns the Harlem neighborhood queries** (`rehab harlem` pos 1.5, `drug rehab harlem` pos 1.2, `addiction treatment harlem` pos 2.1). Three high-volume queries are saturated at avg Maps rank ≤4 (`dual diagnosis treatment centers nyc` 1.98, `inpatient drug rehab nyc` 3.42, `inpatient alcohol rehab nyc` 3.39).

The lift now comes from **three structural moves that compound the won positions** rather than starting new pushes:

1. **Split the homepage** — currently ranking pos 11–21 for 9 commercial NYC queries with 17,100 combined monthly volume on a single URL. Splitting unlocks 9 independent page-1 candidates.
2. **Build borough organic hubs** — 1,727 imp/90d for Brooklyn / Queens / Bronx queries currently bleeding at page 5+. Single-pin Maps reach is capped at the 6-mile Harlem grid; organic SERP is the only path to borough capture.
3. **Lock the climbing surfaces** — `/suburb/upper-east-side` at pos 24 for 13,200 vol, `methadone clinic harlem` already pos 9.7, and the MAT/Suboxone-adjacent blog at pos 16–22 for 15,300 vol all need depth + commercial pull-through.

Asymmetric play this quarter: **don't expand the surface area, deepen what's already winning.** No new Maps work needed. Everything else is on-page architecture and content depth.

## 5. What's Next — 5 Strategic Pushes

Ordered by leverage. Each push is data-justified and measurable in 60–90 days. **Every action in these pushes is enumerated as a specific row in four supporting sheets:**

- `Elev8_Pages_Action_List.csv` — 23 per-page actions (BUILD / UPGRADE / REFRESH / 301)

Tasks in `Elev8_Q2_2026_Tasks.csv` reference specific rows in each sheet.

### Push 1 — Split the homepage into a 9-page NYC landing-page suite

**Why:** Homepage ranks pos 11–21 for 9 commercial NYC queries totaling 17,100 monthly volume. A single URL cannot rank top 5 for 9 different commercial intents simultaneously.

**Action:** Build 4 dedicated landing pages for the highest-volume query clusters — Drug Rehab NYC, Alcohol Treatment NYC, Drug Treatment NYC, Rehab Centers NYC. Then refocus homepage on brand + clinical overview.

**Expected outcome (60d):** Each split page reaches pos 4–8 on its primary query; homepage continues to defend brand queries. Aggregate Tier B volume capture moves from ~0% to ~25–40%.

**Specific actions:** Pages_Action_List rows `PA-01` through `PA-05` (5 builds/rewrites). Internal_Linking_Map rows `IL-01` through `IL-12` (homepage nav + footer + cross-links).

### Push 2 — Build 3 borough organic hubs (Brooklyn / Queens / Bronx)

**Why:** 1,727 imp/90d across 8 borough queries currently route to homepage or weak hyperlocal pages and rank pos 45+. Maps cannot deliver these queries from one pin — organic SERP is the only path.

**Action:** Build 3 borough hub pages with borough-specific clinical access, transportation, sub-neighborhood mentions, dedicated FAQ. Tie to existing /suburb/* sub-pages via internal-link template.

**Expected outcome (90d):** All 3 hubs rank top 20 within 60d, top 10 within 90d on primary borough query. Capture moves from 0 → ~30% of existing 1,727 imp.

**Specific actions:** Pages_Action_List rows `PA-06` through `PA-08`. Internal_Linking_Map rows `IL-13` through `IL-36` (each borough hub gets ~6–11 inbound + outbound internal links). GBP-01 (fix Astoria OR typo) is a prerequisite for BH-02 Queens hub — Maps signal alignment.

### Push 3 — Build the MAT / Methadone / Suboxone service hub

**Why:** GBP description features MAT but no dedicated service page exists. `/blog/suboxone-strips-vs-suboxone-pills` ranks pos 16-22 for 15,300 vol on MAT-adjacent intent. `methadone clinic harlem` ranks pos 9.7 organically on 138 imp without an on-site MAT page. MAT-intent traffic is reaching the site with no commercial pull-through.

**Action:** Single `/services/medication-assisted-treatment` hub covering Methadone + Suboxone + Buprenorphine + Vivitrol. Internal-link from the suboxone blog and the homepage. Refresh 4 informational blog posts with commercial-page links.

**Expected outcome (60d):** MAT hub captures `methadone clinic harlem` from pos 9.7 → top 5; suboxone blog pushes from pos 16-22 → page 1; opens entry path to all MAT-substance queries.

**Specific actions:** Pages_Action_List row `PA-09` (build) + `PA-10` through `PA-13` (4 blog refreshes). Internal_Linking_Map rows `IL-37` through `IL-49`. **Also requires GBP-02** — adding MAT/Methadone/Suboxone/Buprenorphine/Vivitrol/Dual Diagnosis as GBP Services entries (currently missing from Services list despite GBP description naming them).

### Push 4 — Lock and extract from the 3 owned Maps queries

**Why:** Dual diagnosis (Maps 1.98), inpatient drug (3.42), inpatient alcohol (3.39) are saturated on Maps. Question stops being "rank higher" and becomes "extract more from rank #2." Dual-diagnosis specifically has no dedicated organic page despite #2 Maps rank — organic surface is unmonetized.

**Action:** (a) Build `/services/dual-diagnosis` hub. (b) Run baseline AI Overview / LLM citation audit across 5 surfaces × 8 queries. (c) Provide owner-side review-prompt template biased toward dual-diagnosis / inpatient / medical-detox language.

**Expected outcome (90d):** Dual-diagnosis page ranks top 30 within 60d. LLM citation baseline established. Owner adopts review prompt template by end of M2.

**Specific actions:** Pages_Action_List row `PA-14` (dual-diagnosis build). Off-page audit task `DD-02`. Owner Ask `REV-01`.

### Push 5 — /suburb/ consolidation + UES depth upgrade

**Why:** 64 /suburb/ pages with 5 confirmed location cannibals. Harlem alone has 3 competing pages diluting authority. UES suburb page underperforms at pos 24 for 13,200 vol despite being highest-leverage single-location organic opportunity on the site.

**Action:** (a) UES page depth upgrade. (b) 5 cannibal consolidations via 301 redirects (7 specific URLs to redirect to 5 winner URLs).

**Expected outcome (60d):** UES page moves pos 24 → pos 10–15. Consolidated Harlem authority strengthens `/suburb/central-harlem-ny`.

**Specific actions:** Pages_Action_List rows `PA-15` (UES upgrade) + `PA-16` through `PA-22` (7 specific 301 redirects). Internal_Linking_Map rows `IL-51` through `IL-58`.
