Connecting your website to your profiles

This is the biggest opportunity we found — and the reason both pins aren't ranking as high as they could. Right now your website and your two Google profiles aren't fully connected:

  • 1.The website is missing the behind-the-scenes code that confirms each location's name, address, phone and reviews to Google — and it still identifies itself as "Maison Moving (Staging)". This is a quick developer fix that helps Google trust and rank both locations.
  • 2.The phone numbers on your website don't match the ones on your Google profiles. Your site shows different numbers than your Tustin and Chino Hills listings — we'd like to confirm the right number for each and make them consistent everywhere.
  • 3.Your Chino Hills profile sends visitors to your homepage rather than your dedicated Chino Hills page. Pointing it to the right page (and adding a map + local phone there) makes that profile stronger.

None of these are big projects — they're mostly one-time fixes, and together they're the highest-impact work we can do this quarter.
