Niagara Recovery: Q2 2026 v2 Strategic Diagnosis

The engine dominates Niagara County core (100% LD cell coverage 0-15mi from pin) but bleeds 41% of branded demand to an old brand variant, and non-branded discovery collapsed 87% over the last year. Five pushes, not site-rebuild — brand canonical, GBP service-area swap, service-page consolidation, location-page break-out, content cleanup.

01Executive Summary

The Newfane engine works. Across the 9 tracked Local Dominator keywords scanned 2026-02-22 → 2026-04-30 on a 13×13 grid at Newfane (158 cells, ~36mi × 36mi span covering Niagara County + the WNY metro fringe), Niagara Recovery sits in 100% of cells within 0-15mi of pin at avg rank #1-2 across alcohol-rehab, alcohol-detox-near-me, addiction-rehab, and addiction-treatment-center. The 4.3★/47-review GBP at 2600 William St is #1-2 in every neighborhood from Olcott through Lockport through Ransomville. The dominance is structural and durable — no defensive engineering required there.

Where the engine stalls. Three quantified drags. First, brand-conflict bleed: in the GBP's own 6-month search log (Dec 2025 – May 2026, 11,019 profile views), "recovery center of niagara" pulled 363 searches against "niagara recovery" + variants at 528 — the old brand still draws 41% of branded demand 6 months in, because the site uses two brand entities (homepage/footer = "Niagara Recovery"; about/admissions/contact/treatments/areas-served titles + LocalBusiness JSON-LD = "Recovery Center of Niagara"). Second, non-branded discovery collapse: GSC last-30d shows non-branded clicks at 47 against 3,967 over 365d — non-branded discovery has effectively halted (87% extrapolated decline) while the 670-post blog continues publishing (22 new posts shipped 2026-04-15 → 2026-05-18, plus 9 new suburb-doorway pages of the same template that v1 said to consolidate). Third, geographic break-out gap: the GBP service area is at the 20-entry cap, with 13 of 20 entries sitting on just 3 ZIPs (7 on 14094 Lockport, 3 on 14008 Appleton, 3 on 14172 Wilson) — of which 11 are redundant micros that consolidate to 2 keeps — blocking entries for the 8 WNY anchor towns where Niagara already has 100% LD coverage and top-3 rank but no entity declaration.

Five pushes, M1-M3. Push 1 = brand canonical sitewide (one entity, one schema, one image, kill the [phone number] placeholder + Buffalo-14215 fabrication). Push 2 = GBP service-area swap (drop 11 redundant micros, claim Lockport + Cambria + Pendleton + Sanborn + Wheatfield + Lewiston + Youngstown + Clarence + N. Tonawanda) + photo blitz 6→25 + weekly Posts. Push 3 = service-page consolidation (10 URLs → 5 via 301 content-merge; MedicalBusiness schema floor; SP rewrites). Push 4 = location-page architecture (BUILD Tonawanda + N. Tonawanda where 404s exist; UPGRADE Williamsville + Cheektowaga + Lockport + NF + Amherst; REDIRECT ~60 best-/most-trusted-/dependable-/premium- doorways to anchor city pages). Push 5 = content cleanup (NOINDEX/410 ~300 off-vertical posts; refresh 5 high-CTR conversion blogs with intake CTAs; pause the suburb-doorway production firehose). Competitive landscape is winnable: Renaissance/BestSelf (2.7★/22), Beacon (3.6★/16), Casa Di Vita (5.0★/3 tiny base), Pathways CHS (3.3★/69) all beatable on ratings + topical density. The unlock is on-page + GBP, not budget — existing off-page rotation continues.
