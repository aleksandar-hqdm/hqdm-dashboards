Why not NOINDEX or 410?

Location pages are a primary HQDM deliverable. Bulk NOINDEX'ing them removes ranking surface — and Google can take 90+ days to drop NOINDEX'd URLs out of the index, so we don't even get fast equity recovery. The corrected approach: every redundant URL gets a small-level 301 to the MAIN canonical (service hub like /modalities-techniques/<service>/ or area page like /areas-served/<town>/). Link equity routes forward; no surface is lost.
