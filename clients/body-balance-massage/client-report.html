Quarter Organic Visitors Organic Leads Lead Capture Rate Context

"Leads" = booking events + form submissions + call/email click intent. Engagement events (GBP listing taps, banner CTA clicks, gift-card clicks) are excluded — those were added to GA4 as "conversions" in Aug 2025 but they're top-funnel taps, not bookings. Lead capture rate (LCR) = leads ÷ visitors. Neither metric represents completed paid sessions — that requires downstream attribution (CallRail call duration/quality + booking-system confirmation feed) which isn't integrated yet.
