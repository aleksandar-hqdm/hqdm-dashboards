TL;DR

Bernie's lost 52.5% of GSC clicks YoY 90d (16,411 → 7,800) — driven by Google's Aug 2025 core update + AI Overview saturation (0-40% → 89% on top 18 kws). The pre-2025 fundamentals held (vet credentials on 10/10 audited URLs). Parallel issue: GSC Coverage 'Crawled - not indexed' grew 1,492 → 2,670 (+79% in 84 days) from URL parameter pollution + duplicate paths + thin taxonomy. Reshuffle had winners: Spot & Tango +2,078% top-10 visibility, pattern now verified. Three observations make Q3 clear: (1) remaining organic traffic converts 3.2× higher (CVR 0.67% → 2.17%); (2) the store ranks for only 59 kws, 75% branded — the 2.49M monthly SV in-SKU universe is the biggest untapped lever; (3) Google won't index ~2,700 of Bernie's pages today regardless of content quality. Six Q3 levers + 45 actions.
