TL;DR — organic is healthy, SERP-side decline isn't hitting revenue

Per Wicked Reports (your source of truth), Organic Search is Bernie's #1 channel and it's healthy — clicks +36% Q1 YoY (May 2026 at a 17-month high, 25,071), attributed sales steady at +5.5% (~$20K monthly revenue), new leads +124% (335 → 749), and the returning-customer base holding firm at ~370–400/month. What Search Console and Ahrefs show is the real SERP-side decline every content brand is seeing — GSC clicks YoY 90d −52.5% (16,411 → 7,800) and AI Overview presence ~89% on top non-brand kws, driven by AI Overviews + Aug 2025 + March 2026 core updates — but per Wicked it has not translated to a click or revenue impact. Parallel issue: GSC Coverage 'Crawled - not indexed' grew 1,492 → 2,670 (+79% in 84 days) from URL parameter pollution + duplicate paths + thin taxonomy. The reshuffle had clear winners — S&T +2,078% top-10 visibility off a PR-stunt pattern verified across 20 head-to-head article pairs (st-pattern/). Q3 plan: 6 levers + ~50 task rows including 10 site-level template upgrades (site-level-upgrades/), top-50 BLOG refresh connected to the Ideal Sitemap workflow (top-50-losers/), and capturing the 2.49M-SV store gap (store-gap/).
