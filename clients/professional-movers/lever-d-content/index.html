AIO-citation-optimized writing checklist (applies to every post)

  • · Question-format H2s ("How much does it cost to move a piano?") — match PAA + AIO parse pattern
  • · Short, scannable, list-format answers under each H2 (50-150 words)
  • · Embedded YouTube video (PM owns or licensed — YouTube is #1 cited AIO source in moving vertical per LLM mini-report)
  • · Reddit-style Q&A section: "What movers in Detroit are saying about [topic]" — pulls in community sentiment
  • · Concrete brand mention: USDOT 266582, MCICC 178368, "45-year Detroit metro" — credibility signals
  • · Schema: FAQPage with the question-format H2s + Article + Author markup
  • · Internal link out to 2-3 commercial pages (Lever C IL-021..040 backstops)
  • · External citation to an authority (BBB rating, BBB profile link, MSP/AMSA ProMover listing)
