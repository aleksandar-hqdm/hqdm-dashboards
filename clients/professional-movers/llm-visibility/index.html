MINI-REPORT · LLM CHANNEL

AI Overview saturation on PM's keyword set: 35% (7/20). PM cited zero times.

DataForSEO live SERP pull on 20 priority queries · 2026-05-22 · per-keyword + per-bucket + content-pattern analysis.

Methodology

20 priority PM queries selected across four buckets: commercial geo (5), commercial service (5), informational where PM already ranks somewhere (5), mid-funnel commercial (5). Each query queried via DataForSEO serp/google/organic/live/advanced with full SERP feature parsing (AI Overview, Local Pack, PAA, Featured Snippet). Results captured 2026-05-22, US default location, desktop device. Total spend $0.10. Raw exports at HQDM/clients/professionalmovers/exports/dataforseo/ai_overview/.

For each query: did AIO trigger? (y/n). If yes, which domains cited? PM's own organic rank position (if in top-30). Whether PM itself was cited inside an AIO. Buckets chosen so we can spot AIO trigger patterns per intent type — and so the content-strategy implications fall out by funnel position.

Commercial service (5/5)

piano movers detroit · hot tub movers · office moving companies · long distance movers detroit · senior moving services

  • · 0 of 5 have AIO. 5 of 5 have Local Pack.
  • · PM ranks: #21, off, off, #29, off.
  • · All five are Local-Pack dominated SERPs — service intent + geo modifier triggers map.
  • → Lever B (SP-01..07 service-page surgery) + Lever A both apply. AIO not yet a threat.

Informational (5/5)

how to pack dishes for moving · how to move a pool table · how to move a refrigerator · how to pack lamps for moving · how to pack fridge for moving

  • · 5 of 5 have AIO. 0 of 5 have Local Pack.
  • · PM ranks: off page-1 on all 5 (yet PM's blog gets thousands of GSC impressions on these — meaning rankings are deep, citations zero).
  • · Citations dominated by YouTube + Reddit + retailer/manufacturer (Whirlpool, Lowes, Home Depot).
  • · Two competitors cited: Allied + Two Men and a Truck (on pool-table). Cool Hand Movers (on tipping).
  • → Lever D (BLG-01..03 refresh) is the right lever. Content format needs to match what AIO cites: video embed + Q&A structure + concrete brand mention.

Mid-funnel commercial (5)

  • · 3 of 5 have Local Pack (best moving company detroit, moving company near me, detroit movers).
