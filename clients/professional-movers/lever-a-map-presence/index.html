GBP-01 · Detroit Woodward claim playbook

Single highest-leverage Q3 action. PM already pin-bleeds to #2.7 average across 6 priority Detroit queries with no managed GBP. A managed pin closes this gap to #1 within 30 days of claim.

  1. Owner authorization (OCI Ask 1) — verify the 1001 Woodward Ave address is current and PM has operational presence (staff, parking, equipment access).
  2. GMB Manage → Claim listing → ownership verification via postcard mailed to office or video verification.
  3. Category: "Mover" (primary) + "Moving and storage service" + "Piano moving service" (matching service-page coverage).
  4. NAP consistency check across BBB, Yelp, USDOT registry, Yellow Pages, citation directory list.
  5. Description rewrite: USDOT 266582 + MCICC 178368 + CVED L19032 license tokens, 3-office overview, "downtown Detroit" + "Woodward" geo tokens.
  6. Products: 6 service categories with geo-diversified descriptions ("Detroit local moving," "Detroit office relocation," etc.)
  7. Photos: 30+ photos at the Woodward office (storefront, fleet, crew, completed moves with permission).
  8. Reviews: import + re-engage clients with Detroit-area moves to leave reviews mentioning Detroit / downtown / Woodward.
  9. Posts cadence: weekly for M1, biweekly M2-M3.
  10. Q3 target: #1 average rank across 6 Detroit-modified commercial queries by end of M2.

GBP-02 · Rochester claim playbook

Structural gap — physical office at 419 Golf View Ln but zero Maps presence on 6 priority queries. Even non-movers (Auto Hauler Exchange, The Delia Group, Service First Logistics) outrank PM in moving SERPs here.

  1. Owner authorization (OCI Ask 1) — confirm office presence + Oakland University proximity (LL-02 partner opportunity).
  2. GMB claim via same verification flow as GBP-01.
  3. Categories + description tuned for Rochester / Rochester Hills / Oakland University relocation context.
  4. Products focus: residential local moving, senior moving (partner with senior communities LL-03), Oakland University student moves.
  5. 30+ Rochester-area photos.
  6. Review re-engagement on past Rochester moves.
  7. Q3 target: top-5 average rank within 60 days of claim (Rochester SERP currently dominated by non-movers — easier displacement than Detroit Woodward).
